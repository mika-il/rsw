Royal Group

One stop service for all customers need.

  • Royal Skyways (RSW)

    Royal Skyways Co., Ltd. was founded in July 2008 (originally incorporated under the name “Royal Aviation Support” in late 2002) offering aviation academy services which including Private Pilot License (PPL) and Commercial Pilot License (CPL). In addition, the company also holding a valid Air Operator Certificate (A.O.C.) as to operate all Royal Group’s fleet. Royal Skyways also provide Ground Handling Services and Air Taxi and Air Charter Services.

  • Royal Airport Services (RAS)

    Royal Airport Services Co., Ltd. was founded in early 2001 to focus on aviation related services and businesses. With demand for aviation service providers on the rise in the region, the company has expanded its services to cover various needs. The company is now offering variety of professional services which ranging from Repair Station to Fixed Base Operator which in line with Royal Group’s key approach toward one stop service for all customers need.

  • Royal Sky (RSC)

    Royal Sky Co., Ltd. was established in February 1995, as a trading company which focusing on selling aviation related products to Thai Military and Government entities. Royal Sky also represents and works with various O.E.M. and O.E.M. authorized dealers / distributors for promoting and selling products and spare parts. Royal Sky has a credible track record backed by several successful projects and achievements with both government and private customers. Royal Sky is actively operates as an Independent Representative of Bell Helicopter, an authorized representative of Hawker Beechcraft.
